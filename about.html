// About Me

Computer Science (Honours) student majoring in Network Security with expertise in IoT engineering and a passion for building secure systems and implementing zero-trust architectures. Currently pursuing Co-Op opportunities for Spring 2026.

As President of Conestoga Cybersecurity Association (CCSA) and active member of WAT.DEV, AWS, and Google Developers Group, I lead workshops on ethical hacking and secure development practices. I've collaborated with University of Guelph and Laurier University to organize student-industry events that bridge academic learning with real-world security challenges.

My work spans from hardware circuit design to secure software engineering, focusing on threat analysis, system reliability, and data integrity in production environments. I specialize in building complex security tools and implementing robust architectures that protect against modern cyber threats.
